Mcleodganj’s iconic shop to shut after 160 years

28 Aug 2020 by Eyygelena Fteras
Report

One of the oldest shops in Dharamshala’s Mcleodganj to shut after 160 years of service to the locals. Nowrojee General Store is one of the oldest general stores in the area and has given beautiful memories to the people visiting. One of the locals said, “We demand that this shop should be declared as heritage property and should be preserved, so that Mcleodganj’s charm is maintained as in the coming time a co-operate building will be constructed and it will lose its importance.”
