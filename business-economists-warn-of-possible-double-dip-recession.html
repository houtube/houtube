Business Economists Warn Of Possible Double-Dip Recession

27 Aug 2020 by Eyygelena Fteras

The National Association of Business Economists on Monday presented a decidedly gloomy assessment of the US economy.
Based on its August survey, NABE says America remains in a deep downturn and is running a serious risk of a worsening recession lasting at least another year.
CNN reports about half of the organization’s members expect that the US gross domestic product won’t return to its pre-pandemic level until 2022.
CNN reports nearly 80% say there is a one-in-four chance of a double-dip recession. More than half say unemployment benefits should be extended.
As for the US federal government’s massive, unprecedented stimulus program? 40% of NABE members say the government hasn’t done enough.
