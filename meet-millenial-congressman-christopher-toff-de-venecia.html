Meet Millenial Congressman Christopher ‘’Toff’’ de Venecia
If only for having dabbled in various and perhaps opposing careers,
Pangasinan 4th District Representative Christopher “Toff” de Venecia takes the cake.
The millennial congressman was a marketing professional, a
columnist, a magazine and newspaper editor, and most recognizably, a theater director and producer all before his first election to the House of Representatives in 2016.
In this video interview, Rep. de Venecia recalls his journey from the theater stage
to the Congress.
