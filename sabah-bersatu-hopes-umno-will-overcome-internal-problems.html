Sabah Bersatu hopes Umno will overcome internal problems

26 Aug 2020 by Eyygelena Fteras
Report

Sabah Bersatu hoped that the state chapter of Umno would solve its internal conflict in time to prepare for the state election.
State Bersatu chief Hajiji Mohd Noor was asked to comment on the disagreement voiced by Umno supreme council member Abdul Rahman Dahlan over the appointment of Sabah Umno chairperson Bung Moktar Radin as elections director for the upcoming state poll.
