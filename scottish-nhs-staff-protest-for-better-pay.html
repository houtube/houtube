NHS Staff Strike For Better Pay
GLASGOW, SCOTLAND - AUGUST 18: NHS nurses stage a protest outside Queen Elizabeth University Hospital after being left out of a public sector pay rise on August 18, 2020 in Glasgow, United Kingdom. NHS staff across Scotland are protesting today demanding an early salary increase after they were not included in the government’s above-inflation coronavirus pay rise for public sector workers because of a three-year pay deal negotiated in 2018
