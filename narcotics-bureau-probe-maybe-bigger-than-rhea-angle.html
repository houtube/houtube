Narcotics bureau probe maybe bigger than Rhea angle

30 Aug 2020 by Eyygelena Fteras

In the latest development in the Sushant Singh Rajput death case, the Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) is likely to probe link between Bollywood and drugs cartel, say NCB sources.
The narcotics bureau probe may be bigger than the Sushant Singh Rajput-Rhea Chakraborty case.
Currently, the NCB is probing the drug angle in the case and their investigation began on August 27. Earlier, the Narcotics Control Bureau of Delhi has registered a case against Rhea and her four accomplices.
The matter of drugs came to light in this case when the ED seized Rhea Chakraborty’s phone and revealed her WhatsApp chats while investigating the financial angle in Sushant Singh Rajput death case.
