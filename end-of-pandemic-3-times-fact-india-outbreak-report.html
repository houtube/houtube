Report

COVID-19 pandemic is set to hit its peak in India in two weeks, according to the latest projections by the Times Fact-India Outbreak Report, a joint effort by Times Network and research and data firm Protiviti.Interestingly, the recent sero surveys conducted in Delhi, Mumbai and Pune have triggered a massive debate over whether some cities in India could already be experiencing partial herd immunity. Delhi Health Minister Satyendar Jain told that first Sero survey showed that over 22% of people from Delhi were exposed to coronavirus.
